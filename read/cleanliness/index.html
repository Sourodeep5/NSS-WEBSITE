Clean India, Green India - NSS Initiative

With a deep sense of social responsibility and commitment towards community service, the NSS volunteers of Gurucharan University successfully organized and carried out a cleanliness drive across the college campus. The initiative aimed to promote awareness about hygiene, environmental protection, and the importance of maintaining clean surroundings in daily life.The volunteers actively participated in cleaning several important areas of the campus, including the Zoology Department, Commerce Department, Biotechnology and Botany Departments, the Examination Cell, as well as the roadside surroundings. With great enthusiasm and teamwork, they collected waste, cleared unwanted materials, and ensured proper disposal, thereby restoring cleanliness and order to these spaces.This drive not only improved the physical environment of the campus but also inspired students and staff to adopt responsible habits and contribute towards building a cleaner, greener, and healthier society.
